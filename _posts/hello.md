## LW-BVR
Light-Water Boiling Vacuum Reactors (c) are steel tanks which are basically big vacuum chambers. Light water is injected into the chambers at very high PSI (Pounds per Square Inch). The water naturally boils without being hot because of being in a vacuum, which lets of steam. The steam is cycled through a turbine which generates electricity. PQAR invented BVRs, and are planning on the first to be built in 2050.
